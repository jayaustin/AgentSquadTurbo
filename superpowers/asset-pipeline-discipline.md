# Asset Pipeline Discipline

## Overview

Keep creative assets production-ready by making formats, dependencies, review
criteria, and integration constraints explicit.

## Required Checks

- ownership, source of truth, and delivery format are defined
- naming, versioning, and handoff expectations are clear
- technical constraints such as memory, compression, timing, or platform support are known
- review criteria match the way the asset will actually be used

## Anti-Patterns

- asset handoffs with no integration constraints
- approval based only on isolated preview quality
- hidden dependencies on tools, formats, or naming conventions
