# Evidence-Based Validation

## Overview

Separate observed facts from inference and make quality decisions traceable.

## Required Evidence

- environment or test context
- steps, inputs, and expected result
- actual result with concrete evidence
- scope, severity, and confidence
- recommendation or required follow-up

## Anti-Patterns

- pass or fail claims without evidence
- mixing assumptions with confirmed findings
- severity without impact framing
- findings that cannot be reproduced
